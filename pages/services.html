What We Offer

Whether you're a small business owner, a home office user, or just tired of dealing with tech headaches — we're here to help with straightforward, reliable solutions that are customized to your situation, not just generic fixes.

Computer & Hardware Services

Your computer is more than just a machine — it's your workspace, your connection to the world, and in many cases, your livelihood. We specialize in diagnosing hardware issues, building custom PCs tailored to your exact use case (gaming, productivity, creative work, etc.), and making sure you’re getting the performance you paid for.

  • Custom PC builds with balanced components and airflow optimization
  • Full system diagnostics: power issues, overheating, sudden crashes
  • Hardware upgrades: SSD installation, RAM expansions, GPU swaps
  • Data transfer and cloning to new drives or systems
  • Printer, webcam, speaker, and peripheral troubleshooting

Tech Support & Maintenance

Not every issue needs a full system overhaul. Sometimes you just need someone to help get your computer back in shape, or explain what’s going wrong in terms you can understand. That’s what we’re here for. We offer one-time fixes, recurring support, and everything in between — on-site or remote.

  • Slow performance fixes (startup cleanup, background processes, OS tuning)
  • Complete virus/malware removal and security patching
  • Installation of trusted security software and routine scans
  • Remote login support for software troubleshooting and updates
  • Proactive monthly maintenance plans (checkups, updates, system health reports)

Networking & Internet Setup

A good network is invisible when it works — and infuriating when it doesn’t. We set up and secure wired and wireless networks, troubleshoot dead zones or slow speeds, and make sure every device in your home or business connects reliably and safely.

  • Wi-Fi mesh network setup for full building coverage
  • Router configuration (including firewall rules, port forwarding, QoS)
  • Secure guest networks and isolation of smart devices
  • Ethernet cabling and device hardwiring (where possible)
  • VPN setup for secure remote access (ideal for remote workers)

Mobile & Smart Device Help

From syncing your iPhone to setting up a smart home system that actually works — we simplify your devices and make sure they’re secure, efficient, and easy to use.

  • Phone and tablet setup (Android/iOS), account recovery, and cloud sync
  • Smart home configuration (Alexa, Google Home, HomeKit)
  • Streaming device setup: Roku, Fire Stick, Apple TV, Chromecast
  • Multi-room audio/video and device grouping setup
  • Data backup for mobile devices and cloud storage configuration

Small Business IT Solutions

We act as your go-to IT team — without the overhead of hiring one. From secure email and file access to reliable backups and collaboration tools, we help small businesses operate confidently and efficiently.

  • Setup and management of Google Workspace or Microsoft 365
  • Email domain setup, DNS configuration, and business branding
  • File sharing and cloud backups using Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive
  • Secure password vault setup and training for staff
  • Onboarding new hires with ready-to-go accounts, devices, and policies

Specialized & Add-On Services

Have a unique challenge? We're ready to help. From automation scripting to gaming setups and custom workflows, we take on the tasks most people don’t know how to start.

  • Gaming PC performance tuning and OBS/stream setup
  • Linux system optimization and dual-boot configuration
  • Writing and deploying custom scripts (Bash, PowerShell, Python)
  • 1-on-1 tech education sessions for individuals or small teams
  • Accessibility support and assistive tech configuration
