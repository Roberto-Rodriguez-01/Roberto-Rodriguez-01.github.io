Cookie Policy

Last updated: 05/01/2025

This Cookie Policy explains how North Byte IT (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, or track how users interact with content.

2. Do We Use Cookies?

Yes, we may use cookies on our site for basic functionality and potential analytics purposes.

Currently, our site is hosted on GitHub Pages and may not place cookies directly. However, third-party tools such as:

  • Formspree.io (for form handling)
  • Google Analytics (if/when implemented)

…may use their own cookies for service tracking or analytics.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

Type Description
Essential Cookies Required for core functionality, such as submitting forms.
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our site (only if analytics are enabled).
Performance Cookies Improve site speed and reliability.

4. How You Can Control Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to help you:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Disabling cookies may impact how our website functions, especially form submissions.
