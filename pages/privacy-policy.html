Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05/01/2025

North Byte IT ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you submit a message through our contact form (via Formspree.io):

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Reason for contacting us

We do not collect other personal data unless necessary to respond to a specific request.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you submit a form via our website. We may use additional tools like analytics in the future.

3. Why We Collect Information

  • To respond to inquiries
  • To improve the website and user experience
  • To provide IT services as requested

4. Third-Party Services

We currently use:

  • Formspree.io to process form submissions
  • GitHub Pages to host our website
  • We may use Google Analytics in the future to collect anonymous usage data

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it:

  • With third-party service providers (e.g., Formspree, GitHub Pages)
  • If legally required

6. Data Retention

Because we do not store user data directly, retention policies are governed by third-party tools such as Formspree. Contact those services for their policies.

7. Your Rights

At this time, we do not store personal data directly. Any requests for data changes or deletion must be made through the services used (e.g., Formspree).

8. Compliance

We do not currently fall under GDPR or CCPA, but we strive to respect your privacy and will honor reasonable data-related requests.
